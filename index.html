Notes is a local-first note app that lets you create hierarchical notes (trees of notes) stored safely in your browser's local storage. No accounts. No cloud lock-in. Fast, private, and simple.

Local-first benefits
  • Private: data stays on your device.
  • Fast: no network latency.

Hierarchical notes

Create notes inside notes. Build trees for projects, outlines, or knowledge. Expand and collapse branches to focus.

Local storage

Notes are saved in your browser's local storage.

Lightweight & fast

Minimal UI that stays out of the way. Focus on writing and organizing, not configuration.

Privacy first

No account required. Everything is kept on your device by default.

Get started

  1. Create a root note (like a folder)
  2. Add child notes to build a hierarchy
  3. Expand or Collapse branches to navigate
